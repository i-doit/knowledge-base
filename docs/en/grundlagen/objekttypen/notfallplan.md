# Object Type: Emergency Plan

The **Emergency Plan** object type documents IT emergency plans and disaster recovery procedures. Emergency plans link critical systems with their recovery measures and responsibilities.

## Usage

- **Emergency Management**: Document emergency plans with procedures and responsibilities.
- **Object Assignment**: Assign emergency plans to the affected systems and services.
- **Contact Assignment**: Record emergency contacts and escalation paths.
- **Compliance**: Use emergency plans as evidence for audits and certifications (e.g., ISO 27001).
