# Object Type: Organization

The **Organization** object type documents companies, institutions, and facilities. Organizations are legal entities and are typically used for customers, suppliers, or internal departments.

## Usage

- **Suppliers and Partners**: Document external organizations as suppliers, manufacturers, or service providers.
- **Person Assignment**: Assign persons to an organization for a complete contact structure.
- **Location Linking**: Record the headquarters and locations of the organization.
- **Contracts**: Link maintenance contracts with the responsible manufacturer or service provider.
