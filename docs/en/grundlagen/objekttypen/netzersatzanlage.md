# Object Type: Emergency Power Supply

The **Emergency Power Supply** object type documents backup power generators and emergency power systems. Emergency power supplies ensure continued operation of the IT infrastructure during power outages.

## Usage

- **Data Center Infrastructure**: Document emergency power supplies as part of the critical infrastructure.
- **Building Assignment**: Assign the emergency power supply to a building or room.
- **Maintenance Planning**: Record fuel reserves, runtime, and maintenance intervals.
- **Dependency Mapping**: Link buildings and racks that depend on this emergency power supply.
