# Add-on & Subscription Center

The Add-on & Subscription Center is the central hub for managing add-ons in i-doit. You can **install**, **update**, and **uninstall** add-ons — provided you have the appropriate permissions.

You can also see at a glance which versions are installed, whether updates are available, and which i-doit version an add-on requires at minimum. Commercial information such as prices and license requirements is also available here.

!!! info "To access the Add-on & Subscription Center, your license must include only one tenant. Additionally, a connection to <https://center.i-doit.com> is required."

You can reach the Center in two ways:

-   Via the login page of the Admin Center
-   Within i-doit by clicking on the avatar and selecting the menu item **Add-on & Subscription Center**

[![Add-on & Subscription Center](../assets/images/de/administration/add-on-and-subscription-center/asc-1.png)](../assets/images/de/administration/add-on-and-subscription-center/asc-1.png)

## Permissions

Under **Administration → Permissions → Add-on & Subscription Center**, [permissions for persons and person groups](../efficient-documentation/permission-management/index.md) can be configured.

| Permission        | Condition                           | Description                                                              |
| ----------------- | ----------------------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **View**          | Open Add-on & Subscription Center   | Allows access to the Add-on & Subscription Center                        |
| **Execute**       | Manage add-ons                      | Allows installing, uninstalling, and updating add-ons                    |
| **Administrator** | Manage license                      | Allows viewing, copying, removing, and changing the license token         |
| **View**          | View add-on information             | Allows viewing add-on information                                        |
